About

The University of Arizona has been my home as I completed both a graduate coding program and a BFA in Visual Communications. These certifications helped me develop an array of programming, animation, and analytical skills that are beneficial in a variety of work environments. My passion to become an animator has lead to an in depth understanding of both the behind the scenes coding aspect and the front end animation creation.

Over the past few years I have studied and gained an intimate understanding in programming skills including but not limited to Visual Studio Code, Adobe Creative Suite and AutoDesk. During this time my knowledge of HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, SCSS, JavaScript, HandleBars, Node.js, MySQL, and React has also grown significantly.
I welcome any challenge and look forward to showing how much value I can bring to the table.
